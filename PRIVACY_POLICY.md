# Privacy Policy for Masjidie

**Last Updated:** 15/02/2026

**Masjidie** ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Location Data

We access your device’s location data to provide core features of the App, including:

- **Prayer Times Calculation:** Accurate prayer times are calculated locally on your device based on your geographical coordinates.
- **Mosque Finder:** To sort and display mosques nearest to you.
- **Qibla Direction:** To determine the correct direction for prayer.

**Note:** Your precise location data is stored **locally on your device** using secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your history to our own servers or store it in a central database.

### B. User-Provided Information

- **Search Queries:** When you use the "Search Location" feature, your text query (e.g., "Paris") is sent to **OpenStreetMap (Nominatim)** to resolve coordinates.
- **Preferences:** We store your app settings (e.g., calculation method, notification preferences) locally on your device.

### C. Device Permissions

The App may request access to the following device permissions:

- **Location:** To calculate prayer times and find mosques.
- **Notifications:** To schedule and deliver Adhan (call to prayer) alerts.
- **Alarms (Android):** To ensure exact timing for prayer notifications.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1.  Provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.
2.  Display nearby mosques and events.
3.  Send you timely notifications for prayers and events.
4.  Maintain your personal preferences within the App.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

- **OpenStreetMap (Nominatim):** Used for converting your location search/address into coordinates.
  - _Privacy Policy:_ [OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)
- **Apple Maps / Google Maps:** When you choose to navigate to a mosque, the App may open your device's default map application.
  - _Google Privacy Policy:_ [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - _Apple Privacy Policy:_ [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- **Expo:** We use Expo to build and update the App. Expo may collect anonymous usage data to ensure the App runs smoothly.
  - _Privacy Policy:_ [Expo Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** The majority of your data (location, schedule, settings) is stored locally on your device using `AsyncStorage`.
- **No Remote Tracking:** We do not track your movement or store your personal data on external servers.
- **Data Retention:** You can remove your data at any time by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App.

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Here, provide details on children's privacy. Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
